Failure and invention are inseparable twins – Jeff Bezos

Not going to lie…

This one is ironic.

I love the notion of breaking a concept down into a maxim and have been capturing my favorites in a notebook.

The idea was that I’d have a purpose-created small notebook to remind me of my favorite maxims.

In my head, I’d have this perfect book of maxims in order… Yet I accidentally skipped a page.

1, 2, 3, 5, 4 🙈

I thought I could fix it with a maxim from Bezos about failure.

It would be sweetly ironic.

Then, when writing it out, I made a spelling mistake.

Perfect 😂.
